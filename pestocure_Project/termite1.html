Termite Pest Control in Hyderabad

Pestocure Pest Control Services are best for treating Termite in your home & also called White Ants living in your home. These Termites are social insects they live underground as colonies. Termites feed on anything containing cellulose like wooden doors, furnitures, fixtures, papers, books, clothes etc. Termites infest and breed underground in the soil. Termites crawl up through the minute cracks and crevices in the foundation and the walls of your building. Termites are hard to detect and are almost always discovered only after the damage has been done.We are one of the best Termite Control in Hyderabad.

Process Involved in Termite Pest Control Service (Drill-Fill-Seal)
  1. Drilling 8MM Dia & 6MM Dia Holes at bottom of inside walls and walls near door & window frames.
  2. After Drilling is done ,Insecticide needs to be filled in drilled holes with pressure pump.
  3. After filling insecticide , all Holes needs to be sealed with White chalk and White Cement.
