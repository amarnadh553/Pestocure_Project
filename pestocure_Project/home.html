Stage 1: Drilling above wall skirting with feet gap of 10mm size inclined holes with 4 to 6 mm depth with hammer drill.
Stage 2: Filling insecticides through pressure pump and injecting insecticide around wooden frames like door, window, cupboard etc... Within the holes provided near to frames or choukats. Spraying Bayer Agenda with Turpent oil on doors, windows, cupboards doors, etc.... For surface protection...
Stage 3: Sealing all the filled holes with white cement to regain its strength and finish of wall.
By carrying all these 3 stages you flat will be protected from Termites upto 3 years
